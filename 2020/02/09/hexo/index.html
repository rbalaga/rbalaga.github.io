Creating a Markdown Blog using Hexo.

Hello, I thought of creating a self designed blog with minimal work. I had did some research on creating a personalized blogs for Tech related posts and which are self hosted. I went to blogger.com and few other providers. But they were hosted on their own cloud. It is most for recommended for non tech people.

I just went though some tech posts and found Hexo! is matching to my requirement where i can write my posts on local computer and host our files and get control over the content.

Let’s go through the steps I did for creating this blog.
